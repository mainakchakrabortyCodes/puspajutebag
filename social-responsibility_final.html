Social Responsibility

Social Responsibility of a company is an ethical framework that suggests a responsibility for a much larger number of people. It is a strategy where companies aim to create a positive impact on society while conducting business. Social responsibility includes obligations towards the general public, investors, employees, workers, clients, environment, and local community. Every company has its own approach.

Param Jute Products is committed to the education of rural children and raising social consciousness through the Indian Creative & Welfare Society. By offering eco-friendly products, we directly contribute to environmental protection. Join us in our mission to make a Greener World.

  • Supporting rural education initiatives
  • Promoting eco-friendly products for a sustainable future
  • Ensuring fair labor practices (no child labour)
  • Using AZO-free colours for printing & dyeing
  • Maintaining high production capacity: 240,000 to 260,000 shopping bags/month
  • Skilled craftsmen and premium quality materials
  • Competitive, fair pricing and instant quotations
  • Multiple shipping options available
