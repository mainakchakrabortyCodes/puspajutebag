Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our products and services.

Are jute bags eco-friendly and reusable?
Yes, jute bags are 100% biodegradable and eco-friendly. They are also highly durable and can be reused multiple times, making them an excellent alternative to plastic bags.
How long do jute bags last?
With proper care, jute bags can last for several years. They are sturdy and can carry heavy items without tearing, making them a sustainable long-term option.
Can jute bags be washed?
Yes, jute bags can be hand-washed with mild detergent. It's best to air dry them naturally to maintain their shape and durability.
Are your bags customizable?
Absolutely! We offer customization options including printing logos, designs, and text on our bags. Contact us for more details on customization options.
Do you offer bulk discounts for large orders?
Yes, we offer competitive bulk discounts for large orders. Please contact our sales team for more information on pricing and order quantities.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept various payment methods including credit/debit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All transactions are secure and encrypted for your safety.
